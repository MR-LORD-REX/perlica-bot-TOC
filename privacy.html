Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data Endfield Bot ("we", "our", or "us") collects from users and servers on Discord, why we need it, and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect minimal data required for the Bot to function. This includes:

  • User Data: Discord User IDs, usernames, and display names.
  • Server Data: Discord Guild (Server) IDs and Server names where the Bot is present.
  • Game Information: Game UIDs, auth tokens, and associated game settings you explicitly provide to the Bot.
  • Bot Settings: Your preferences, such as language settings, profile templates, and reminder toggles (e.g., daily/weekly reminders).
  • Usage Data: Temporary cache data for rendering profiles or cards, and command execution metadata for rate-limiting and analytics.

Note: We do NOT log or store the content of your messages unless you are explicitly interacting with a Bot command that requires it.

2. Why We Collect This Data

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide core bot functionalities (e.g., linking your game account to your Discord profile).
  • Send scheduled reminders or notifications that you have opted into.
  • Maintain server-specific configurations and moderation settings.
  • Improve the Bot's performance, fix bugs, and prevent abuse.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. We employ reasonable security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Passwords or highly sensitive authentication tokens are handled securely and, where applicable, encrypted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your data is strictly used for the internal operations of the Endfield Bot. We will only disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Bot and its users.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is kept as long as you use the Bot. If you wish to have your data removed, you can typically do so via the Bot's account removal commands (if provided) or by contacting the developers. Temporary cache data is automatically deleted after a short period.

6. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates on Discord and relies on the Discord API. Please refer to Discord's Privacy Policy to understand how Discord handles your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy or this policy, please join our official support server and reach out to our team.
